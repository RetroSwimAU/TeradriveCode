บ  This is the other half of the MegaDrive ROM I released   บ
บ  a few weeks ago. We put some text on the VDP screen,     บ
บ  and are playing the music on the PSG on an interrupt.    บ
บ                                                           บ
บ  If the TeraDrive's rear switch is in the RGB position,   บ
บ  you should see the MegaDrive side output on composite.   บ
บ                                                           บ
บ  If it's in the Video position, the PC output should be   บ
บ  showing right now.                                       บ
บ                                                           บ
บ  Press SPACE to toggle the output between VGA and VDP.    บ
บ  In RGB mode, the composite output sticks to VDP.         บ
บ                                                           บ
บ  Got a MegaDrive controller plugged in to port 1?         บ
บ  You should try pressing down on the Dpad!                บ
